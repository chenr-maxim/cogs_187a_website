About Me


Front End Web Developer from San Francisco.

Currently I go to school in University of California San Diego. I have a passion for design, art and illustration. In order to continue my passion for design, I am majoring in Cognitive Science - Human Computer Interaction. As an HCI major, I have had experience with Web Development and UI/UX design.

Experience with UI/UX and Web Development.

During the summer of 2016, I worked on a small team of Web Developers on the iOS application, PianoNow. Helped develop the front end and back end parts of the application and integrate features such as user accessibility services and searches. Using JavaScript, AngularJS, HTML, CSS, helped program and integrate different core features into the application.

Then the summer of 2017, I worked as an intern in a UI/UX Web Development team at ChangeHealthcare. I worked with the team to create different parts of their online web portal for printing documents, and information about invoices and the interactions with hospitals and patients. Using React, Javascript, I helped fix and integrate different functionality to the website.

Future Endeavors.

With experience creating web applications and having an eye for design, I hope to continue illustrating, and creating visually appealing projects. The more experience I have with front end, I hope to continue building on that foundation and eventually become a proficient full stack web developer. Not only with web technology, I hope to perfect my artistic ability with graphic and design.
